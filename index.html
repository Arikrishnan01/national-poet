Robel Poet

The Rebel is a 30 lines poem in which the poet talks about the characteristics of a rebel in the society. A rebel is one who does everything against the norms of society. In the poem, the poet tells us that a rebel is usually hated by people but it is usually good to be one because it makes one different.
